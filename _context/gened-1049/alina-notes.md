# gened1049 East Asian Cinema resource 

## takeaways from each station

### lighting 
* Take test shots with your phone before shooting. You may not always be able to control the lighting (for example, in the dorm or outside) but you can reposition the actors and use your photo to take test shots at different angles. 


### shooting dialogue
* One-camera shooting: how to shoot one actor's lines first then shoot the other actor
* Work with two phones: shoot with one phone and use another as a mic positioned between the two actors
